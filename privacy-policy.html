Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Godasya ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or place an order. This policy is published in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, the IT (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA).

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to process your order and communicate with you. The table below summarises what we collect and why:

Data Collected Purpose Legal Basis
Name Order processing & delivery Contractual necessity
WhatsApp / Phone number Order confirmation, dispatch updates, support Contractual necessity
Delivery address Shipping your order Contractual necessity
Order history Subscription management & dispute resolution Legitimate interest
Website usage data (IP address, browser, pages visited) Analytics, security, improving user experience Legitimate interest
Order details (product, quantity, batch) Product safety, recall management & regulatory compliance Legal obligation

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial account numbers, payment card details (payments are handled directly via UPI/bank transfer), government IDs, passwords, or health records.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm, process, and dispatch your orders
  • To send order status and delivery updates via WhatsApp
  • To manage monthly subscriptions and bulk orders
  • To respond to queries, complaints, and refund requests
  • To send occasional promotional messages about new products or offers (you may opt out at any time by messaging us "STOP")
  • To issue GST invoices where requested, using your name, delivery address, and GSTIN
  • To notify you of any product safety issue or recall, and to identify and contact affected customers as required under the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To improve our website and services

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. WhatsApp & Third-Party Services

Orders are processed via WhatsApp, which is operated by Meta Platforms, Inc. By messaging us on WhatsApp, you agree to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy. We do not control the data practices of WhatsApp or Meta.

Our website may be hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., a Microsoft subsidiary). GitHub Pages may collect basic server logs. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

We use Google Fonts (Google LLC) to load typefaces. Google may collect limited technical data. See Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes stated in this Policy:

  • Order and transaction records — retained for 6 years in accordance with the Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 and applicable accounting and legal compliance requirements
  • WhatsApp message history — retained as per WhatsApp's own retention policies; we may refer to past messages for up to 2 years to resolve disputes
  • Customer contact details — retained until you request deletion, subject to our legal retention obligations
  • Website analytics data — anonymised or deleted within 12 months

5. Your Rights Under DPDPA 2023

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to AccessRequest a summary of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to CorrectRequest correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to EraseRequest deletion of your personal data (subject to our legal retention obligations)
  • Right to WithdrawWithdraw consent for promotional messages at any time by messaging "STOP"
  • Right to NominateNominate another person to exercise your rights in the event of your incapacity or death
  • Right to GrievanceFile a complaint with our Grievance Officer (see Section 6) or with the Data Protection Board of India
